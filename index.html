Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the strengths of your company?

    We have trusted employees with skills and experience such as:

    ‣ Design Skills: NFPA Specialist, Professional Engineers and Senior Draftsman
    ‣ Installation Skills:
            • Project Manager with more than 30 years of experience
            • Welders and Pipe fitters with more than 10~30 years of experience
    ‣ Trading Skills: IMPEX and sales teams partnered with well-known Korean company

  • From where are your products made and how am I assured of its quality?

    We import certified products from Korea, India, UAE, China and US etc. They all meet the required standard like the ISO 7165: 2009, ISO 9001, NFPA 10, ASME Codes and Standards were followed during the construction and manufacturing of the projects.

  • What specific brands do you offer?

    We offer brands like Tyco, Vikings, KITZ, Globe, Daeyoung Power Pump, Masteco, Yooksong, Wondae TGK, Asenware, and Leaders-tech for different fire protection equipment. They all carry specific certification to ensure their quality.

  • What are the requirements for requesting a quotation?

    Technical Specification and commercial requirement such as:

    1. Definition of the products or services required with detailed specifications
    2. Delivery requirements
    3. Quantities
    4. Payment terms
    5. Contract terms and conditions
    6. Submission requirements

  • What inspection and consultation services do you offer?

    We conduct any inspection and consultation services with regards to fire protection that is being required by the customer. We have NFPA specialists, professional mechanical and electrical engineers, who will provide you cutting-edge solutions for your requirements. For inquiries you can e-mail us at info@ajssent.com.

  • How fast it will take to accomplish a project?

    We efficiently manage the project schedule based on customer requirement. Wet Stand Pipe System takes 60 to 90 days regardless of its size. Automatic Fire Suppression System may depend on the design and external factors.

  • I couldn't find an item on your website that I am looking for. Can you get it for me?
